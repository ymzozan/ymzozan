## Civic technology & social impact

Beyond commercial e-commerce, I apply the same engineering discipline to civic technology — products that turn national initiatives into measurable, data-driven engagement.

* **[Sıfır Atık Hub](https://github.com/ymzozan/sifir-atik-hub)** — Designed and engineered an AI-powered, gamified zero-waste platform for schools, built as a working prototype presented to the **Sıfır Atık Vakfı (Zero Waste Foundation)**. Camera-based waste recognition, real-time impact analytics (CO₂ saved, recycling, reforestation), and inter-school gamification — leagues with promotion/relegation, sports tournaments and awareness quizzes — with a foundation analytics dashboard. Architected end-to-end with Next.js, TypeScript and Tailwind CSS. ([Live demo](https://sifir-atik-proje.vercel.app))
